Our mission

We aim to “develop student's interest in the business analytics major and industry” by providing a common and open environment for students interested in Business Analytics. to connect with different industries, participate in social and professional events, along with meeting new friends and having personal access to lecturers and academics.

Our history

Sydney University Business Analytics Association (SUBAA) is an officially sponsored Sydney University association designed to create a comprehensive environment for students studying or interested in business analytics. Founded in 2018, SUBAA works with the business analytics faulty, Sydney University (USYD) Business school and key company partners for students to learn about the various business analytic industries.

What we do

The association hosts professional development events, case competitions and workshops to connect students with industry partners. SUBAA also provides opportunities for students to learn and develop fundamental skills related to business analytics via workshops and study groups hosted by the association and USYD business school.
