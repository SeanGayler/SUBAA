Business Analytics Major

Business Analytics is a subject area provided by Sydney Universities Business School, which can be undertaken as a major or minor within the faculty. Business Analytics teaches students quantitative methods to utilize in the various industries that use business analytics and business data for company operations. The subject area provides students with skills to understanding, extract and summarise rich data sets and operational models to create data-driven solutions, decisions and insights. The Business Analytics subject also enhances and compliments a range of electives such as business statistics, engineering, operations management and decisions sciences.

More information can be found in the USYD Business School Handbook.
